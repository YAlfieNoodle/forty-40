• Forty Forty Rules

You can play this game with unlimited people.

One person trying to catch out the rest of the players.

How to play:

As a group, you establish the spot where "homey" will be.

The person who is on will count, with their eyes closed, for 40 seconds.

The other players will hide in/around surrounding obstacles, and will have to run to "homey" without getting caught and when they touch it they have to say "Forty forty home".

The person who is up can catch people by calling their names out after "Forty forty I see..."

The person who is up has to be touching "homey" to catch someone.

The fact that you have to be touching "homey" to get someone out, can cause some EPIC races between the person who is up and the person trying to get home!
